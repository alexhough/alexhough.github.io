Dropbox drops hosting service

Dropbox has changed its policy on hosting HTML files. This means that OMM is moving to a new home using GitHub Pages

On Sept 1 2016, TiddlyWiki inventor Jeremy Ruston was the bearer of bad news for TiddlyWiki users who have been hosting TiddlyWiki websites on Dropbox for years.

OMM was on dropbox for 8 years....

The good news is that you can still use TW with Dropbox, just not as a public website.
